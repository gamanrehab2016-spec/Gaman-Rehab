Our Services

We provide a wide range of rehabilitation services to help patients recover and regain their independence

Neuro Rehabilitation

Comprehensive neurological recovery for conditions such as brain injury, spinal cord injury, and neurological disorders.

Key Features:
• Motor function restoration
• Balance and coordination training
• Cognitive rehabilitation

Stroke Physiotherapy

Specialized therapy for stroke survivors to regain mobility, strength, and independence.

Key Features:
• Gait training
• Upper limb rehabilitation
• Speech and swallowing therapy

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Treatment for musculoskeletal injuries and conditions to restore movement and reduce pain.

Key Features:
• Joint mobilization
• Post-fracture rehabilitation
• Sports injury management

Post Surgery Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation after surgery to promote healing, restore function, and prevent complications.

Key Features:
• Early mobilization
• Scar management
• Strength and flexibility training

Cardio Respiratory Physiotherapy

Therapy for heart and lung conditions to improve endurance, breathing, and overall health.

Key Features:
• Pulmonary rehabilitation
• Cardiac conditioning
• Airway clearance techniques

Long Term Acute Care

Specialized care for patients with complex medical needs requiring extended recovery and rehabilitation.

Key Features:
• Multidisciplinary team approach
• Complex wound care
• Ventilator management

Tracheostomy Care

Expert care and rehabilitation for patients with tracheostomy to ensure safe breathing and recovery.

Key Features:
• Airway management
• Suctioning and cleaning
• Decannulation support

Occupational Therapy

Helping patients regain independence in daily activities through adaptive strategies and skill development.

Key Features:
• Daily living skills training
• Cognitive rehabilitation
• Adaptive equipment training

Speech Therapy

Therapy to improve communication, speech, and swallowing abilities for all ages.

Key Features:
• Speech and language training
• Swallowing therapy
• Voice therapy

Respiratory Therapy

Specialized care for respiratory conditions to improve lung function and breathing efficiency.

Key Features:
• Oxygen therapy
• Airway clearance
• Breathing exercises

Geriatric Care

Comprehensive care for older adults to maintain independence, mobility, and quality of life.

Key Features:
• Fall prevention
• Mobility training
• Chronic disease management

Elderly Care

Supportive care for elderly patients focusing on comfort, dignity, and holistic well-being.

Key Features:
• Personal care assistance
• Medication management
• Social and emotional support
